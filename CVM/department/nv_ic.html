Industrial Chemistry Program Details

Industrial Chemistry at NVPAS (Natubhai V Patel College of Pure and Applied Science) is a three-year undergraduate program that focuses on the practical application of chemical principles in various industries. The course aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills to transform raw materials into commercially useful products. It includes both theoretical and practical components, with subjects like inorganic chemistry, polymer science, and biotechnology. NVPAS also offers industrial visits and training to enhance understanding of classroom learning. Key Aspects of Industrial Chemistry at NVPAS: Focus: The program emphasizes the application of chemical principles in industrial settings, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, petrochemicals, and plastics. Curriculum: It covers topics such as unit operations, unit processes, plastics and polymers, dyes and drugs, safety, instrumentation, and pollution control. Practical Experience: Students engage in practical work, including laboratory experiments, and industrial visits to gain hands-on experience. Career Prospects: The program prepares students for various roles, including research scientist, plant in-charge, lab chemist, and entrepreneur. Industrial Training: NVPAS provides industrial visits and training to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industrial applications. Additional Support: NVPAS offers tutorial classes, remedial classes, and guidance for postgraduate studies through its Entrance Exam Preparation Cell (EEPC). Personality Development: The college provides a personality development program and encourages participation in extra-curricular activities to foster well-rounded individuals.
