Description

Charutar Vidya Mandal University (CVM University), established in 2019 in Vallabh Vidyanagar, Gujarat, is a private university with a strong lineage tracing back to 1945 under the Charutar Vidya Mandal Trust. It's recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. The university is known for its focus on research and innovation, aiming to foster a culture of creativity and leadership among its students.
